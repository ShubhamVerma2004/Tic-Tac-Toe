Quantum Tic-Tac-Toe Guide

Welcome to Quantum Tic-Tac-Toe! This game puts a quantum twist on the classic game you know.

How to Play

  • You play as X against the Quantum AI which plays as O
  • Click on any empty cell to place your X
  • The first player to get 3 in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins
  • If all cells are filled without a winner, it's a draw (Quantum Entanglement!)

AI Difficulty Levels

  • Novice: Makes random moves - great for beginners
  • Advanced: Sometimes blocks and sometimes makes random moves
  • Expert: Uses advanced algorithms to always make the best move
  • Quantum: Uses quantum-inspired probabilistic moves - unpredictable!

Features

  • Toggle between dark and light mode
  • Track your stats including win rate and current streak
  • Sound effects for moves and game outcomes
  • Responsive design works on all devices

Good luck and enjoy the quantum experience!
